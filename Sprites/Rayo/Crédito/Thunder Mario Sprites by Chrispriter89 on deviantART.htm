New Power up Suit, Thunder Mario.
The Thunder Flower Gives Mario and Luigi the ability to Shoot Thunder Balls, unlike the Fire balls and Ice balls, the Thunder balls don't bounce.

Enemies get Paralyzed for a short time, when Paralyzed Mario and Luigi can pick an enemy up and throw them at another enemy.

Also Mario and Luigi can do a Thunder Ground Pound to release a Thunder that will strike at a random spot, hopefully it can directly strike an enemy
